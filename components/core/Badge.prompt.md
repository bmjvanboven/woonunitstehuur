Compact status pill for availability and state. Soft tint by default, `solid` for filled.

```jsx
<Badge tone="success" dot>Direct beschikbaar</Badge>
<Badge tone="gold" solid>Populair</Badge>
<Badge tone="danger" dot>Verhuurd</Badge>
```

Tones: `neutral` · `gold` · `success` · `info` · `warning` · `danger`. Props: `solid`, `dot`.
